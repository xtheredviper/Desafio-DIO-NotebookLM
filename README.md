# 🤖 The Ghost in the Machine: Philosophy, Ethics & Cyberculture

This repository is dedicated to exploring the human-machine relationship through the lens of classical philosophy, critical theory, and dystopian media analysis.

---

## 📌 Core Themes

* **Cyborg Ontology:** The blurring of boundaries between the biological and the artificial.
* **Algorithmic Ethics:** Asimov's Laws and the moral alignment problem.
* **Post-Humanism:** The evolution of human identity in high-tech environments.
* **Socio-Technology:** Inequality, surveillance capitalism, and the atrophy of empathy.

---

## 🏛️ Critical Theory & Ethics

> Fundamental references that underpin the theoretical debate of this project.

* **Isaac Asimov:** The Three Laws of Robotics and the dilemmas of ethical programming.
* **Donna Haraway:** *A Cyborg Manifesto* – Hybrid identity and the breakdown of binaries.
* **N. Katherine Hayles:** *How We Became Posthuman* – The tension between information and embodiment.

---

## 🎮 Media Analysis (Case Studies)

### 🧠 Consciousness and Identity
* **Ghost in the Shell:** The duality between the "Ghost" (soul/data) and the "Shell" (prosthetic body).
* **Ergo Proxy:** The *Cogito* virus and the existential quest for *Raison d'être*.
* **Cyberpunk 2077:** The commodification of the soul through the "Relic" and the continuity of consciousness.

### ⚖️ Politics and Rights
* **Detroit: Become Human:** The struggle for machine civil rights and the master-slave dialectic (Hegel).
* **Blade Runner:** The replicant as the "Other" and the fragility of human memory.

### 📉 Inequality and Dystopia
* **Cyberpunk: Edgerunners:** The physical and mental cost of technology (*Cyberpsychosis*) in hyper-capitalist societies.
* **High Tech, Low Life:** Technology as a tool for social stratification and the erosion of empathy.
